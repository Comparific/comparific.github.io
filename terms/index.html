Privacy Policy

Comparific compares thousands of combinations of price plans and optional services. In order to do this whinin reasonable time, the analysis is done on our servers. This means that the application needs to collect information about your phone usage.

What information is automatically collected in the background?

In order to perform an accurate price plan comparison our algorithm needs to know about your call history, SMS history, MMS history and data usage. More precisely we collect:

  • Which numbers you have dialed or sent SMS/MMS messages to
  • The time at which you have made a call or sent a SMS/MMS message
  • The time and duration of your calls
  • How much data has been sent/received

Nothing else (apart from data manually entered in the application) is collected or uploaded to our servers. In particular we do not collect any recording of your calls, content of the SMS/MMS messages or what data is being sent and received.

What of the above is stored on our servers?

Only information important to future analyses is stored on our servers. This includes for instance the time and duration of each phone call, the country and operator associated with each phone numbers but not the phone number itself.

Manually entered data

Information manually entered in the application, such as settings, email address and phone number is stored and uploaded to our servers for a better user experience.
